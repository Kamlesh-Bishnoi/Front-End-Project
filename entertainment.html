Confirmed: 'Zero' trailer to release on Shah Rukh Khan's birthday, 2nd November :-

Shah Rukh Khan's upcoming 'Zero' is one of the most anticipated movies of the year. The teaser of the film was released this year on Eid which had a cameo from Salman Khan. Director Aanand L Rai recently confirmed the news that full-fledged trailer will be released on November 2, the day that marks the birthday of Shah Rukh Khan.
